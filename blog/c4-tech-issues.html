Sunday: Diversity

March 22, 2015

Gender At Major Tech Companies:

Amazon Male(63%) Female(37%)
Apple Male(70%) Female(30%)
Facebook Male(69%) Female(31%)
Google Male(83%) Female(17%)
Twitter Male(70%) Female(30%)
The stats above indicate that gender imbalance is prevalent throughout the tech industry but it does not have to be this way. If we commit to the following practices, we would at least be headed in the right direction towards permanent change.

  • Expose girls and women to STEM careers specifically software development i.e. coding
  • Increase hiring initiatives for women
  • Increase support for women owned startups
