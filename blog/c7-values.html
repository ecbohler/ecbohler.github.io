Thursday: Affirmation and Stereotype Thread - My Values

April 9, 2015

My Values

  • Confidence
  • Honesty
  • Volunteering
  • Accountability
  • Committment
  • Independence
  • Knowledge
  • Music

Accountability is a very important value. It ensures that I take responsibility for all of my decisions whether they yield positive or negative returns. When I am accountable, I am forced to think through the possible outcomes of my decisions and how they will impact others. Accountability builds character and is essential in becoming a great leader.

I feel empowered when reflecting upon my values. I believe that I can accomplish anything regardless of the stereotype threats that exist. Knowledge, Committment, Honesty and Confidence are the cornerstones of my excellence and I believe these will help me mediate the feelings that stereotype threats might bring if that in fact occurs.
