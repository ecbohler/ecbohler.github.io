Week of Sept 9/14-9/21 - Reading 2015

Although I have a true passion and genuine interest in technology, coding and engineering...I've also gotten quite fascinated with wealth and money management. My husband and I have had plenty nightly conversations about our life and what do we see as truly being happy. We quickly realized that having the financial ability to travel the world and help others was what we identified as being happy. In order to get to this place, we know that we need serious, cold hard cash and we need to be debt free.

So I've decided to read about wealth, money, investing, retirement, business, startups, etc. in order to get into the frame of mind of the like and I'd like to share this with you all as well.
